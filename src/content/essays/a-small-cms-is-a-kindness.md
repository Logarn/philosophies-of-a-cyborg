A CMS should be boring in the best possible way.

It should let me write, pin, publish, and leave. It should not ask me to become a product manager for my own sentence.

## Robust does not mean huge

Robust means the few important things do not fall apart.

That is the bar. Not a cockpit. Not a maze. A desk.
