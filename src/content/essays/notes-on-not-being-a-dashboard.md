I keep coming back to the same annoying truth: most software wants to look serious before it has earned the right.

So it adds charts, tabs, filters, and a sidebar with the emotional range of a spreadsheet.

This site is not trying to do that. It can just be a room with a few objects in it.

## A room is enough

You click a thing. You read a thing. You leave or you do not.

That is already more honest than half the internet.
