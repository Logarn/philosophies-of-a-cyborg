Cute does not have to mean soft, beige, and helpless.

The best interfaces can smile with teeth. They can be playful without becoming childish, precise without becoming sterile, and opinionated without burying the user under preferences.

This site should feel like that: light, tactile, slightly game-like, and allergic to blandness.
