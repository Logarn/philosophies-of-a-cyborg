Agents do not need a cute button.

They need text. Routes. Links. A page that says what exists without making them crawl through a fake human experience.

So the site gets a mode for that.

## Polite to machines, useful to people

The funny part is that agent-readable pages often help humans too. Clear text has a way of doing that.
