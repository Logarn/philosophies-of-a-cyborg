Most portfolios are too clean.

They pretend everything arrived fully formed and made perfect sense the whole time.

Project OS should not do that. It should show the useful mess: what exists, what is broken, what is almost good, what should maybe be thrown away.

## A shelf, not a shrine

That is the metaphor. A shelf can hold unfinished things without lying about them.
