The internet has too many heroes that say nothing very loudly.

Big headline. Soft gradient. Three vague nouns. Button. Button. Floating screenshots of a product nobody can touch.

I would rather have a desktop with some files on it.

## Objects beat slogans

A strange little icon can say more than a paragraph of startup confidence.
