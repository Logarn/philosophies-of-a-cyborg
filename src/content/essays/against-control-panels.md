Most admin interfaces are built by people who confuse possibility with responsibility.

They add a button because the system can do a thing. Then another. Then a dropdown. Then a settings page. Then one day you open the tool and feel like you need clearance from air traffic control to publish a paragraph.

## Three buttons is a philosophy

Publishing needs fewer doors.

For this site, the important actions are brutally obvious: publish the work, pin the best work, and keep editing the draft that is not ready yet.

Everything else can wait outside and think about what it did.
