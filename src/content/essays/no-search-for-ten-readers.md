Search is useful when the archive is large enough to punish memory.

Before that, search is often a costume. It says: this place is vast, indexed, and important. But if ten people are reading, the honest job is simpler. Put the best essays at the top. Put everything else below. Make the page fast. Make the titles good.

Let people scroll. Scrolling is not a crime.

## Pins beat algorithms

A pinned post is an editorial act. It says: start here.

That is more valuable than a search box on a site whose archive can still fit in a lunch break.
