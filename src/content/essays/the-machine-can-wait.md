The machine is very good at making everything feel immediate.

New message. New task. New build. New dashboard. New little red dot begging for a tiny piece of your nervous system.

I am trying to remember that most things can wait.

## Waiting is not laziness

Sometimes waiting is the only way to find out whether an idea has legs or was just caffeine wearing a costume.
